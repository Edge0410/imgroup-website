About us

Challenges & Solutions

From routine and preventative maintenance to performance enhancements and modernizations, you can depend on IMGgroup to analyze your operations, assess the condition of the equipment, and advise how to improve the efficiency and performance of your plant, including the flue gas treatment system. All from IMGroup.

To complement our services, IMGroup manufactures a full range of replacement pressure parts. IMGroup specialists can assist in shut-down planning, on-site coordination, supervision, and the actual shut-down work, as well as monitoring, troubleshooting, and optimization of boiler operation. Challenge IMGroup to put our experience, technology, and knowledge to work at your power generation plant.
